## How we measure website traffic

We use a privacy-friendly analytics tool to understand how visitors use this
website so we can improve it. It is designed to respect your privacy:

- **No cookies.** We do not store cookies or any persistent identifier on your
  device for analytics.
- **No personal data.** We do not collect or store your IP address, name, email,
  or anything that identifies you personally.
- **No cross-site or cross-day tracking.** We cannot follow you across other
  websites, and we cannot recognise you when you return on a different day.

### What we measure

Aggregated, anonymous statistics only, such as:

- Which pages are viewed, and how many times.
- Approximate location at **country / region / city** level (derived from your
  IP address momentarily and then immediately discarded — we never store it).
- Device type, browser, and operating system.
- Where visits came from (e.g. a search engine or social network) and the length
  of a visit.

This information is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you.

### Your choices

If your browser sends a **"Do Not Track"** signal, we do not measure your visit.
